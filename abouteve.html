Rules & Regulations

Do's and Dont's

Rules to be followed by the participantss

(click on the points to get more Information about it)

Do's

Remember to bring your own devices, such as laptops and chargers, for a seamless coding experience. Wifi will be Available.

Get ready for an exhilarating overnight event filled with innovation and creativity. Prepare for creating the worthy competition to others.

Respect and support your fellow participants, fostering a friendly and collaborative atmosphere. Else, neccesary strict actions would be taken against the respective team.

Show sportsmanship and grace, celebrating the achievements of others and encouraging each other's growth. Maintain code of conduct.

Don't hesitate to seek help from our dedicated volunteers whenever needed throughout the event. We are always at your service.

Collaborate with your team members to tackle challenges and create innovative solutions together. Learning good team management abilities.

Take breaks for relaxation activities to stay refreshed and focused during the hackathon. Relaxation is required for this overnight event.

Ensure you follow all guidelines provided by the organizers to ensure a smooth and successful event. Else, neccesary strict actions would be taken against the respective team.

Adhere to safety guidelines and precautions while using electronic devices and equipment. Don't hesitate to bother the volunteers incharge for assistance.

Dont's

Strictly refrain from engaging in any form of plagiarism to uphold the integrity of the event. Else, neccesary strict actions would be taken against the respective team.

Maintain a respectful and focused atmosphere by avoiding disruptive behavior or activities. Else, neccesary actions would be taken against the respective team.

Please do not bring tiffins to the event, as food will be provided along with snack stalls always available. Reach out with volunteers incase of assistance.

Ensure you do not use any prohibited or illegal software/tools during the hackathon. Else, neccesary strict actions would be taken against the respective team.

Abide by fair play principles and avoid using any unfair means to gain an advantage. Else, neccesary actions would be taken against the respective team.

Do not use unauthorized means to access or modify the event's infrastructure or network, as this could compromise the integrity of the event and create security risks.

Kindly refrain from leaving the college premises during the event for a seamless experience. As it is strictly not allowed to do so for safety purposes

Avoid seeking help from external individuals to ensure a level playing field for all participants. Else, neccesary actions would be taken against the respective team.

Respect the college amenities by refraining from tampering with them and maintaining their proper use. Else, neccesary strict actions would be taken against the respective team.

What is BVP Hackathon'23?

Detailed Information about the Grand Hackathon

  • Get ready for an exhilarating journey at BVP Hackathon - an overnight coding marathon that runs non-stop, fueling your passion for technology and innovation.
  • To ensure a seamless experience, we offer comfortable accommodation within the college premises, allowing you to stay fully focused and immersed in the hackathon.
  • Join a vibrant community of participants from both degree and diploma colleges, where diverse minds come together to exchange skills and ideas, enriching the hackathon experience.
  • Be prepared for thrilling challenges as the problem statements are unveiled on the event day. You'll have the freedom to choose any sources to tackle them, showcasing your technical prowess and creativity.
  • Put your coding skills to the test as you design and develop both front-end and back-end solutions for the given problem statements. It's an opportunity to demonstrate your full potential and leave a lasting impact.
  • For more Information contact us from the Live Chat box at the bottom right. We are always there to assist you!
  • Come, be a part of BVP Hackathon, and let your innovation and talent shine in this tech extravaganza!
