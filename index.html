About The Code Catalyst Hackathon 2023

The CodeCatalyst Hackathon is an exciting and innovative event that brings together talented students, developers, and technology enthusiasts from diverse backgrounds in computer science. Organized by the Hackathon Committee at Bhausaheb Vartak Polytechnic, Vasai, our mission is to foster a culture of creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving in the world of technology.
