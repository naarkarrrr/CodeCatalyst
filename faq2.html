FAQ's

  • Frequently Asked Questions aboutBVP Hackathon'23
What is BVP Hackathon'23?
BVP Hackathon is an overnight coding marathon organized by Bhausaheb Vartak bringing together tech enthusiasts for a thrilling two-day hackathon to showcase their technical skills and creativity.
When and where will the event take place?
The event is scheduled to take place on September 9th and 10th 2023 (tentative), at the premises of Bhausaheb Vartak Polytechnic in Vasai.
Who can participate in BVP Hackathon?
BVP Hackathon welcomes participants from both degree and diploma colleges, fostering diversity and skill exchange.
How are teams formed for the hackathon?
Participants can form teams of four students, and each group can include members from different colleges.
What kind of problem statements will be provided?
Problem statements will cover various domains, including technology, social impact, sustainability, and education, offering a wide range of challenges. And will be declared on the event day itself i.e.: 15th August, 2023
Can participants use any sources to tackle the problem statements?
Yes, participants are encouraged to use any sources they find relevant to solve the given problem statements.
Is it an overnight event?
Yes, BVP Hackathon is an over 24h event which will last overnight, providing an immersive coding experience for participants.
Will food be provided during the event?
Yes, food will be provided for all participants throughout the event. Additionally, food stalls will also be available for everyone with vast range of snacks.
Are there any rules and regulations participants should be aware of?
Yes, participants are expected to follow the event's guidelines, which can be viewed on our website's Rules&Info section. Also would be shared during the event briefing
How will the winners be selected?
The top 10 teams will be selected based on their performance and the quality of their solutions. The final winners will be announced by a panel of esteemed judges.
Is there any accommodation provided for participants coming from outside Vasai?
Yes, accommodation will be provided within the college premises for outstation participants to ensure a comfortable stay.
Can I participate as an individual, or do I need to be part of a team?
Certainly can't, as each team should include atleast 2 members, but we encourage forming teams of four students for a more collaborative experience.
Will there be mentors or experts available during the event?
Yes, experienced mentors and experts will be available to guide and support participants throughout the hackathon. Also, appointed committee members and volunteers would be present as well incase of any help.
What kind of problem statements will be provided?
Problem statements will cover various domains, including technology, social impact, sustainability, and education, offering a wide range of challenges. And will be declared on the event day itself i.e.: 15th August, 2023
What should I bring to the event?
Bring your own devices (laptops, chargers, etc.) to work on the problem statements. Other essentials like personal belongings should also be brought. Preferabbly avoid bringing tiffins or snacks to the event as it is strictly not allowed. Additional Food and snacks stalls would be present within campus.
Can I change teams after registration?
Team formations are final after registration, so ensure you have the right team members before signing up. Incase of any mistakes in entries contact Team BVP Hackathon23 mentioned on the website's contact page.
Are there any prizes for the winners?
Yes, the winning teams will be awarded exciting prizes, certificates, and recognition for their exceptional solutions. The Prize details are to be announced soon. Stay Tuned!
How can I stay updated with event announcements and information?
Regular updates, event schedules, and guidelines will be shared through email, website and our official social media handles. Join us by subscribing our newsletter or contact us via our website's contact page. Stay tuned.
Is there any registration fee for participating in BVP Hackathon?
Yes, The Registation Fees are 200INR per team. It's an inclusive event for all tech enthusiasts. Check out the Fee Structure on our website via Highlights >> Entry Fee Structure.
How do I register for BVP Hackathon?
To register, visit our website and fill out the registration form with your team details and contact information. Hackathon Registation is associated with Devfolio.
